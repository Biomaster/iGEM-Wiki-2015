About the Team

Ophir Ospovat , 2017

President

Ophir is a Junior majoring in Biomedical Engineering, Neuroscience, and Molecular & Cellular Biology. As President, Ophir is responsible for overseeing the different divisions of the team, including planning projects, fundraising, managing team resources, and outreach.

Stephen Lee, 2017

Vice President

Stephen is a Junior majoring in Biomedical Engineering. As Vice President, Stephen conducts lab work for the team, manages team resources, and assists with organizing team events.

Sikandar Raza, 2018

Treasurer

Sikandar is a Sophomore majoring in chemistry and neuroscience, As Treasurer, Sikandar is responsible for team fundraising and ordering lab supplies, in addition to conducting lab work.

Jarrod Shilts, 2017

Director of Research

Jarrod is a Junior majoring in Neuroscience and Molecular & Cellular Biology. As Director of Research, Jarrod is responsible for leading the team’s synthetic biology division, including planning projects, designing and running experiments, coordinating lab work, managing lab space, and training members.

Daniel McClanahan, 2017

Director of Software

Danny is a Junior majoring in Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, and Math. As Director of Software, Danny is responsible for leading the team’s software division, including planning projects, writing programs, and coordinating coding work.

Anna Hwang, 2017

Director of Design

Anna is a Junior majoring in Computer Science. As Director of Design, Anna is responsible for the graphic design of the team’s official wiki pages, drawing figures, and designing team logos, banner and other paraphernalia.

Will Cox, 2017

Director of Mathematical Modeling

Daniel Shaykevich, 2018
